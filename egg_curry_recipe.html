Egg Curry Recipe

This is a simple recipe on how to prepare Egg Curry. For a start, someone may ask what the health benefits of Egg Curry is.

Health Benefits of Egg Curry

  • Source of Vitamin B12
  • Source of healthy Fat
  • A rich source of healthy Protein

Ingredients Required

  • Two eggs. Ensure that they are boiled and cut into halves or quarters.
  • Two finely chopped onions Two finely chopped tomatoes.
  • Two finely chopped cloves garlic Finely chopped ginger.
  • One spring curry leaf ¾ teaspoon of Garam masala.
  • ¼ teaspoon of coriander.
  • ¼ teaspoon of black pepper powder.
  • ¼ teaspoon of turmeric (Haldi) One stalk of finely chopped Dhania (coriander leaves.
  • ¼ cup of water.
  • Two tablespoons of cooking oil.
  • Salt.
